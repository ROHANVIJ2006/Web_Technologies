Frequently Asked Questions

1. When does the new batch start?

New batches for all courses start in April and June every year. Crash courses have monthly batches.

2. Is there an entrance test for admission?

Yes, for JEE and NEET courses, we conduct a diagnostic test. For foundation courses, admission is based on previous academic performance.

3. Do you provide study materials?

Yes, comprehensive study materials are provided for all courses, which include textbooks, practice books, and test papers.

4. Are online classes available?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes. Students can choose their preferred mode of learning.

5. What is the batch size?

We maintain small batch sizes of 10-15 students to ensure personalized attention.
